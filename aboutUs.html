ABOUT US

OFFERING SWEET MOMENTS TO EVERYONE

At Baked For Fun, we want to delight you. We want you to experience the warmth of a fresh baked cookie, the joy of a spoonful of tortes or the decadence of a creamy filled cupcake. We want to remind you how good simple can be - the creaminess of real butter, a dash of pure rich vanilla, chunks of finely crafted chocolate. With every bite, a sweetness worth savouring.

Whether helping you choose the perfect gift or plan a special surprise, we're with you every step of the way.From just because to just married, we believe every moment is worth celebrating.

We take care of every detail of your order so that you can focus on what matters most - enjoying life's sweet moments!

WHAT WE OFFERING

At Baked For Fun,we believe every day needs a little something special. We also believe that 'special' shouldn't be complicated. So that's why we spend our days focusing on the simple things: a SHMOO tortes, heavenly filled cupcakes, chocolate strawberry mousse cakes. All made from scratch by our skilled bakers, all crafted with the best ingredients, all ready to transform an ordinary day into an occasion.

Attention to the tiniest details ensures that your dessert, and your experience is fabulous, each and every time.With gift delivery coast to coast, our goal is to spread joy across Canada, one fresh baked gift at a time!
